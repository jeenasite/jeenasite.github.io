About The deep

Chosen Text Rationale

The deep was chosen as the origin of the excerpt text due to it being a multi-genre book. It can be categorized as the following genres:

  1. Fantasy
  2. Mystery
  3. Thriller
  4. Horror

With its multi genre approach, it captures a few of the moods identified in the design outline for the website. This will provide a distinct and visually striking view of the text depending on the genre of the current text being read. As per the requirements of the project, this text will allow for the expression of emotion/ mood while remaining engaging to the reader.
